About Us

From Mexico to Sydney. It all started with a craving for real Mexican food - the kind that tastes like home. La Taqueria Mexican Street Food is a space to share the flavours of our land and the food we grew up with. Our traditional recipes have been passed down through our abuelas (grandmothers), straight from our family kitchen, inspired by the vibrant markets and traditions of Mexico. Today, Gables is our home, and we're excited to bring the real flavours of Mexico to our community, our food is honest, simple, fresh and full of flavour, just the way it's meant to be. We're proud to introduce a taste of Mexican culture, using as many local ingredients as possible and supporting local farmers along the way. Bienvenidos!
